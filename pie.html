Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie



  • One 9-inch pie
  • 30 Min - prep time
  • 10 Min - Cook time
  • 40 Min - Total
  • 8 Servings

INGREDIENTS


  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbspTable spoon all-purpose flour
  • tpsTea spoon cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons,juiced and zested
  • 4 eggs yolks,beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp Table spoonwhite sugar

  • 3 ) Make Meringue:In a large glass or metal bowl
